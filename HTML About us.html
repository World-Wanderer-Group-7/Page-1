The World Wanderer Team

Here is how it all started...

A team of four with a single goal - To create a website helping women travel the world together safely! We would like to create awareness of our home countries through providing travel advice and information about them and the attractions they offer. Alongside this, we are also keen to help women feel secure when travelling around the world since we understand the dangers and unpleasant situations they have been draged into.

Galena

She is a travel enthusiast since the age of 6! A native Bulgarian, Galena has graduated in London, UK and will continue her studies in Barcelona, Spain. She loves reading books about adventures and histroty which is the main drive for her passion for traveling.

Khizara

Khizara claims to be a foodie fanatic who enjoys delving into the deep culture that our world has to offer. She loves excursions, bubble tea and local chicken shops.

Mia

Mia is a Doctoral researcher in Econometrics and will soon work in Tech. She loves travelling and would like to share a positive image of her beautiful home country - Vietnam to the World.

Tsvety

Tsvety is an ambitious, positive and energeting young woman. Her part-time job as a fashion model gives her the opportunity to travel the world which she says is priceless. Her love for Italy is driven by the delicious Italian cuisine, the ancient architecture and the challenging fashion industry.
